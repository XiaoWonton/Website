Who am I?

I'm a speech pathologist looking to make a career change and learning to code.

What is speech pathology?

Speech pathology is a large scope of practice that includes communication, cognitive-linguistic, voice, and swallowing disorders.

Check out our professional oragnization here for more information and resources: ASHA

Why do you want to make a career change?

I feel like I have explored the various options in the SLP field and am looking for change. I want to continue challenging myself and learning new things.

Why should you be considered for software engineering roles?

I have completed college level CS classes (e.g., introduction to java, introduction to linux) and have a passion for continuing to develop my skills to become software engineer. My career as an SLP has allowed to develop transferable skills including team management, time management, and effective communication.

What projects have you completed or are working on?

I have completed up to week 9 of Harvard's online CS50 course and am working on a text based RPG game in java. I am also planning on making a database of food consistencies for SLP's and their patients.
